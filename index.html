About us

Springs of Life Evangelistic Minisitry (SOL)

Vision

To take the gospel of Jesus Christ to places where the light of God's knowledge is dim, where the gospel has not been heard and the power of God not known

Mission

We honor God by planting spirit empowered, Christ centered and social relevant churches and schools in every nation.
