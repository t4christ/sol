About us

Welcome to Springs of Life Evangelistic Ministry

Vision

To take the gospel of Jesus Christ to places where the light of God's knowledge is dim, where the gospel has not been heard and the power of God not known

Mission

We honor God by planting spirit empowered, Christ centered and social relevant churches and schools in every nation.

We Beleive...
The Scriptures

The bible is the inspired word of God, (II Timothyn 3:15-17, I Peter 1:23-25)

Water Baptism

Baptism in water is a phusical action symbolizing a believer's putting off of the old, sin nature and bieng identified wiht Jesus' death and resurrection and newness of life of the believer. (Matthew 28:19; Romans 6:4; Acts 10:47-48.

The GodHead

There is one true living God made up of three persons: The Father, The Son, and the Holy Spirit. All are co-equal and co-eternal. (John 1:1-5, 10-14; Matthew 28:18-20)

Communion

Communion is an act of remembrance of Jesus' body being broken and His blood being shed for the sins of the world. (I Corinthians 11:24-25, 28)

The Fall

God created man in His image, perfect and without sin. God created man for fellowship wiht Him, but man revelled against God and took on the nature of sin. (Genesis 1:1-5, 10-14; Matthew 28:18-20)

The Church

The Churchis the Body of Christ made up of people redeemed by Christ. (Ephesians 1:22-23; 2:19-22; Hebrews 12:23)

Salvation

Sin seperated man from God, and man's only hope of redemption is through the shed blood of Jesus Christ (Romans 3:24, 10-18; 10:8-10)

The Baptism of the Holy Spirit

This wonderfu experience is distinct from and subsequent to the experience of the new birth. It is th e infilling of the Holy Spirit that brings the ppwer of God to a believer. (ACts 2:38, 10:44-46, 19:2; Galatians 5:16-26)

Healing

Jesus too all of our sickness, disease, and infirmities into His own body on the cross so that we would not have to bear them

Jesus Return

Jeusu is coming lback to earth to gather those who have been born again to live with Him forever. (I Corinthians15:51-52; I Thessalonians 4:16-17)

Sanctification

Eternal Punishment

Anyone who has not recieved Jesus Christ as their personal Lord and Savior will be forever seperated from God and will recieve eternal punishment wiht the devil and all his followers. (Revelation 19:20; 20:10-15)
